Place an Order/ Track an Order

Here, you can place an order or track a current order. To place an order,

  1. Go to Orders
  2. Click on 'Place an Order'
  3. Fill out the order form and click submit
  4. Follow the mailing directions from there!

To track an order,
  1. Retrieve the tracking number from your confirmation e-mail
  2. Go to Orders, then click 'Track an Order'
  3. Enter your tracking number into the order search bar
  4. Your order should appear with the latest information!
    5. If order has been shipped less than 12 hours ago, tracking information may not have updated yet.
